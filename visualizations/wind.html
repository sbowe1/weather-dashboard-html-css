Wind Speed

This is a scatter plot illustrating the relationship between latitude and wind speed in mph. Like the humidity graph, data points appear randomly placed in relation to the latitude, and also are congregated around a specific region. However, because there is no pattern within the data, there is a very low correlation between the two variables. Unfortunately, data is not available for latitdues under -60°.
